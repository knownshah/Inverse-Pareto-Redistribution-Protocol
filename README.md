## Core Mechanism — Inverse Pareto

1. Participants deposit a fixed amount (e.g. $10 USDT) into a smart-contract pool each cycle.  
2. A provably fair on-chain oracle (Chainlink VRF) randomly selects winners:  
   - **80% probability**: Immediate return of principal + 20% gain ($12 cash back).  
   - **20% probability**: No immediate cash, but receive a tradable **token**.  

3. The token lives on the open market with full price discovery and upside potential.  
4. In strong cycles, token appreciation pushes effective win rate to 90–95%.  
5. A natural **4% buffer** remains after every cycle and is used for automatic buybacks and staking rewards. (open for best agentic optimazation suggestions)

## Token Value Accrual (Natural & Organic)

- **Gate 1 (Protocol)**: 80% winners receive immediate profit and are motivated to re-invest a portion into the token → organic buy pressure.
- **Gate 2 (Open Market)**: Token is available on DEX/CEX like any other token → viral hype from confirmed winners drives natural buying.

The 4% buffer creates built-in long-term demand through automatic buybacks and staking rewards.
