## The Problem
Current financial markets are hyper-Darwinian. 80–90% of participants lose money over time while a tiny minority captures almost all gains. The majority consistently funds the success of the few.

## Humans + AI Working Together
Many fear AI/agents will take jobs and widen inequality. This flips it: Humans + AI uplift each other. Agents handle the heavy work. Humans get reliable modest wins + governance/token ownership.

## Solution — Inverse Pareto
Participants deposit a fixed amount (e.g. $10 USDT) into a smart-contract pool each cycle.  
A provably fair on-chain oracle randomly selects:  
• 80% → immediate +20% cash back  
• 20% → tradable token (second-chance asset)

The token lives on the open market. Strong cycles push effective win rate to 90–95%.

## Utility
80% of participants win modestly and reliably every cycle — something no meme or token has ever delivered consistently.
