Today’s financial markets and token launches are overwhelmingly zero-sum or negative-sum for the majority of participants:

- Retail traders in crypto lose money in 80–90% of cases over 12 months.
- 98% of token launches lose most or all of their value within 6–12 months.

## 2. The Ideological Core
This protocol is a conscious intervention to break that cycle.

It injects a new reward function into the Grand Algorithm — one where the **majority is designed for positive outcomes**, agents have a noble and sustainable role, and the system becomes self-reinforcing rather than self-destructive.

## 3. Our Solution — Inverse Pareto
Participants deposit a fixed amount (e.g. $10 USDT) into a smart-contract pool each cycle.  
A provably fair on-chain oracle randomly selects:

- **High probability (~80%)**: Immediate return of principal + 20% gain ($12 cash back).  
- **Lower probability (~20%)**: No immediate cash, but receive tokens instead.

## 4. Economic Transparency (Pool Math)
Example with 100 participants ($10 each):

- Total pool = $1,000  
- 80 participants receive $12 each = $960 paid out  
- 20 participants receive tokens  
- Natural 4% buffer = $40 retained
